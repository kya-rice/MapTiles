Lab 4- Map Tiles
This map displays 4 seperate maps. One map is the blue monochrome basemap. Another one of the maps is a street map of abandoned roads in Washington State. Another map is the monochrome basemap with the abandoned Washington roads data from the street map layered on top of it. And the final map is a theme map of the abandoned roads in washington.You are able to toggle between these 4 different maps using a toggle function.
